  • Provitamin B5

    Provitamin B5 has an excellent ability to bond to the hair and add more structure to each strand by adding layers of resilience and strength. Additionally, it helps prevent moisture loss and helps condition the skin under the eyebrow & lash making it the perfect surface for growth.

  • Ubiquinone

    Also known as Coenzyme Q10 - this enzyme has anti-aging effects on human hair through activation of specific keratins needed for the strength of new and mature hair. This coenzyme is essential to helping prevent any old or new hair from falling out or becoming damaged.

  • Wheat Amino Acids

    Wheat Amino Acids are a blend of amino acids that come from wheat protein. It is able to penetrate the hair and deliver moisturizing and conditioning benefits to protect and repair the very important keratin. This results in an increase of strength, condition, elasticity, and flexibility of the hair.

  • Arginine HCL

    Arginine HCL helps slow hair loss by increasing the level of nitric oxide in the body.

  • Soy Amino Acids

    Soy Amino Acids are able to improve the texture and strength of hair that has been damaged or lost over the years. It is incredible for re-hydrating your hair and gives it that beautiful shiny look.

  • Vegetable-Derived Squalene

    Vegetable-derived squalane nourishes the skin underneath brows and eyelashes to aid in brand new root growth. It also has a great ability to replenish strands of hair to protect it from further damage.
